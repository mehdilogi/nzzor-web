# Nzzor — Image Credits & Sources

This file documents the source and license of imagery used on Nzzor. Keep it
up to date as photos are added or replaced — it protects against future
licensing questions and helps anyone onboarding to the project understand
where each asset comes from.

---

## Hero image (homepage)

**File:** `public/hero-courtyard.jpg`
**Painting:** "Court of the Harem" (Cour du Harem)
**Painter:** Paul-Albert Girard (1839–1920), French Orientalist
**Setting depicted:** Dar Hassan Pacha — the 18th-century palace built in 1791
in the Casbah of Algiers, Algeria. Still standing today and part of the
UNESCO World Heritage Casbah of Algiers.
**Approximate date:** c. 1880

**License:** Public domain. The painter died in 1920, well beyond the
copyright term in all relevant jurisdictions.

**Source of reproduction:** Wikimedia Commons
https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Court_of_the_Harem_by_Paul-Albert_Girard.jpg

**Editorial treatment:** The image on the site is a color-enhanced version
of the public-domain reproduction, cropped to 16:9 and with a gentle
top-darkening overlay for headline legibility. Standard editorial latitude
for public-domain works.

**Credit on the live site:** Bottom-right of the hero, displayed in three
languages (EN/FR/AR), reads e.g.:
> "Court of the Harem" · Dar Hassan Pacha, Algiers
> ALBERT GIRARD, C. 1880

## Other imagery

### Hotel photos
Hotel photos should always come from one of:
1. Photos taken by the hotel itself, supplied through the partner portal
2. Photos taken by Allouni staff during onboarding visits
3. Licensed stock photography with documented provenance

**No unverified Unsplash/Pexels photos on production.** All hotel imagery
must be traceable to a real source. This protects the brand promise of
authenticity.

### Painting/heritage imagery
Algerian heritage imagery (paintings, historical photographs, public-domain
works) is permitted in marketing contexts when:
- The underlying work is in the public domain
- The setting depicted is verifiably Algerian
- The work is properly credited (painter/photographer, title if known, location)
- The source of the reproduction is documented in this file

### Logos and brand assets
The Nzzor logo, color palette, and typography are proprietary to Allouni
Travel Agency and not licensed for external use.
